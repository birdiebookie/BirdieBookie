🟢 Total Putts

Every putt matters. One cold putter can destroy an entire gambling round.

How Total Putts Works

Players track total putts during the round.

Any shot played ON the green counts as a putt.

Important:

  • Putts from the fringe do NOT count unless the ball is on the green
  • Using a putter off the green does NOT count as a putt
  • Any club used on the green DOES count as a putt

Lowest putts wins:

  • Front 9
  • Back 9
  • Total 18

Every missed short putt becomes gambling pain.

Why Golfers Love Total Putts

Easy To Track

Simple scoring with nonstop pressure.

Short Putts Matter

Three-putts become financial disasters.

Perfect Side Action

Works alongside almost any gambling game.

BirdieBookie Tip

Before the round decide:

  • Dollar amount per section
  • Whether ties push or carry
  • If gimmies count as one putt
  • Whether handicaps apply

Total Putts quietly becomes one of the most stressful gambling games on the course.
