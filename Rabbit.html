🐇 Rabbit

Catch the rabbit and keep it alive. One hot streak can own the entire round.

How Rabbit Works

The rabbit belongs to the player who wins a hole outright.

Once a player has the rabbit:

  • The rabbit stays alive until another player wins a hole outright
  • Each carried hole increases the rabbit value
  • Long streaks create massive payouts

Example:

  • Player wins Hole 3 = catches rabbit
  • No outright winners on Holes 4 and 5
  • Rabbit value keeps growing
  • Another player finally wins Hole 6 = steals rabbit

Why Golfers Love Rabbit

Huge Pressure

Players protect the rabbit aggressively once payouts build.

Massive Momentum Swings

One birdie can completely flip the gambling action.

Late Hole Drama

The rabbit becomes more valuable the longer it survives.

BirdieBookie Tip

Before the round decide:

  • Starting rabbit value
  • Carryover rules
  • Whether pars can steal rabbit
  • Birdie bonus payouts

Rabbit becomes extremely dangerous when nobody can win a hole outright for several holes.
