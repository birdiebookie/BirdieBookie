😈 Demon

One player becomes the Demon each hole. Beat the group or pay the price. Pressure-packed golf gambling.

How Demon Works

One player is assigned as the Demon for the hole.

The Demon competes against the rest of the group and must beat everyone outright.

  • If the Demon wins the hole → collects from all players
  • If the Demon loses → pays all players
  • Ties usually push unless house rules say otherwise

The Demon rotates each hole so every player takes a turn under pressure.

Popular Demon Variations

Double Demon

Par 3s or Par 5s are worth double payouts.

Birdie Bonus

Birdies by the Demon earn extra payouts.

Carryover Demon

If nobody wins, the pot rolls to the next hole.

BirdieBookie Tip

Demon works best with aggressive players and fast gambling momentum.

Before the round decide:

  • Dollar amount per hole
  • How ties work
  • Whether birdies double payouts
  • Carryover rules
