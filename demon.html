😈 Demon

Three matching scores creates the Demon. Sometimes players intentionally miss putts to save money.

How Demon Works

Demon is triggered anytime 3 players finish with the same score on a hole.

The 4th player either pays or gets paid based on the score difference.

Example:

  • Three players make 5
  • Fourth player makes 7
  • The 4th player owes EACH player 2 chips

Another example:

  • Three players make 5
  • Fourth player makes 3
  • Each of the 3 players owes the winner 2 chips

The larger the score difference, the bigger the payout.

🔥 The True Demon

If 3 players make true Demon scores:

6 • 6 • 6

The payout DOUBLES.

This is called:

The Mark of the Beast

True Demon holes create chaos late in gambling rounds.

Why Golfers Love Demon

Insane Pressure

Players sometimes intentionally miss putts to avoid becoming the Demon.

Big Money Swings

One bad hole can explode into massive chip losses.

Perfect Gambling Drama

Short putts become terrifying when Demon is active.

BirdieBookie Tip

Before the round decide:

  • Chip value
  • Whether Demon doubles only on 666
  • Maximum chip losses
  • Whether birdies count double

Demon creates some of the strangest pressure situations in golf gambling.
