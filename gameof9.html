9️⃣ Game of 9

Every hole is worth 9 total points. Simple scoring. Brutal gambling pressure.

How Game of 9 Works

Game of 9 is played with 3 golfers.

Every hole awards a total of 9 points:

  • Lowest score = 5 points
  • Second lowest = 3 points
  • Highest score = 1 point

At the end of the round:

  • Add all points earned
  • Highest point total wins

Tie Rules

If players tie, their points are combined and evenly split.

Example 1:

  • Mark and Sonny tie with pars for best score
  • 5 + 3 = 8 points
  • They each receive 4 points
  • Terry finishes last and still receives 1 point

Example 2:

  • Mark wins hole with a birdie = 5 points
  • Sonny and Terry tie for second
  • 3 + 1 = 4 points
  • They each receive 2 points

Example 3:

  • All 3 players tie the hole
  • Each player receives 3 points
