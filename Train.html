🚂 Train

One of the most dangerous momentum games in golf gambling. The train keeps rolling until somebody stops it.

How Train Works

Every hole adds value to the train.

When a player or team wins consecutive holes, the train keeps growing.

  • Win 1 hole = start the train
  • Win again = bigger train
  • Keep winning = massive payouts

The longer the streak continues, the more dangerous the game becomes.

Breaking the train usually wins the accumulated pot.

Why Golfers Love Train

Huge Momentum Swings

One bad stretch can destroy an entire round financially.

Pressure Builds Fast

Every hole becomes bigger as the train grows.

Perfect Gambling Drama

Players start protecting leads and chasing trains aggressively.

BirdieBookie Tip

Before the round decide:

  • Starting train value
  • Maximum train size
  • Carryover rules
  • Whether birdies double the train

Train gets especially dangerous late in the round when pressure and money both increase.
