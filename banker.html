BANKER

How to Play

Before the round begins, each golfer selects one hole on the front nine and one hole on the back nine as their Banker holes.

Only the score made on your Banker hole counts toward this game.

After everyone finishes the selected hole, compare scores.

  • Lowest score wins the pot for that Banker hole.
  • Ties split the pot unless your group plays carryovers.
  • The process repeats on the back nine Banker hole.

Example

Player A chooses Hole 4.
Player B chooses Hole 6.
Player C chooses Hole 8.
Player D chooses Hole 9.

Each selected hole becomes that player's Banker hole. Lowest score on each Banker hole wins.
