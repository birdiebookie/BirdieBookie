🎯 Bingo Bango Bongo

One of the most competitive and underrated golf gambling games ever created. Every hole has 3 points available.

How Bingo Bango Bongo Works

Each hole has 3 separate points:

  • Bingo = First player on the green
  • Bango = Closest to the pin once everyone reaches the green
  • Bongo = First player to hole out

Players earn points throughout the round and the highest total wins.

Playing order matters heavily and strategy becomes important around the greens.

Why Players Love It

Everyone Stays Alive

Even weaker golfers can win points during the round.

Constant Action

Every hole creates multiple chances to win money.

Great Pressure

Short putts and approach shots become high-stakes moments.

BirdieBookie Tip

Before the round decide:

  • Point value
  • Tie rules
  • Whether honors matters
  • Whether Bingo requires being first from off the green

Bingo Bango Bongo works especially well with mixed skill groups.
