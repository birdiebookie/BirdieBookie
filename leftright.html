How Left Right Works

  • After each tee shot, the player farthest left and the player farthest right become partners.
  • The two remaining players become the opposing team.
  • Teams change every hole based on tee shot position.
  • Chicken: $0.50 per player.
  • Boring: $1 per player.
  • The Man: $5 per player.
  • Big Dog: $10 per player.
  • Swinging Dick: $20 per player.
  • Winning team members collect from each losing player.
  • If both teams tie the hole, it is a push and no money changes hands.
  • Front 9, Back 9, and Total games may all be played at the same time.
  • Presses and carryovers can dramatically increase the action.
  • The bigger the bet, the bigger the stories in the clubhouse.
