🎯 Game of 11

Choose your scores wisely. Only 11 holes count at the end of the round.

How Game of 11 Works

After every hole, each player decides:

Will this score count toward my final 11 holes?

Players can only select 11 total scores during the round.

  • Good early scores create pressure later
  • Bad early choices can destroy the finish
  • Late birdies become painful decisions

At the end of the round:

  • Add together the 11 chosen scores
  • Lowest total wins

The strategy becomes balancing safe scores versus saving room for potential birdies later in the round.

Choose... But Choose Wisely

Example:

  • A player takes a 5 on a Par 5 early and decides to keep it
  • Later they birdie 17 and 18
  • But they already selected all 11 holes
  • The birdies cannot count

That creates nonstop strategy and second-guessing throughout the round.
