📊 Quota Rules

Each player gets a personal Quota number based on handicap.

Points per hole:
• Double bogey or worse = 0 pts
• Bogey = 1 pt
• Par = 2 pts
• Birdie = 4 pts
• Eagle = 6 pts

Add up your points for the round, then subtract your Quota.
Positive = you beat your Quota (you win). Negative = you fell short.
