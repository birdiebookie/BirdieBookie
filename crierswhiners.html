😭 Criers & Whiners

Complain your way around the golf course. Strategic mulligans create gambling chaos.

How Criers & Whiners Works

Players receive mulligans based on handicap.

These extra shots can be used anytime during the round.

  • Higher handicaps receive more “cries”
  • Lower handicaps receive fewer
  • Unused mulligans usually disappear after the hole

Players must decide when to:

  • Replay drives
  • Retry approach shots
  • Save bad holes

The strategic use of mulligans becomes part of the gambling battle.

Why Golfers Love It

Massive Strategy

Knowing when to use mulligans changes the entire round.

Great Equalizer

Higher handicaps stay competitive deep into the round.

Wild Momentum Swings

One replayed shot can flip huge bets instantly.

BirdieBookie Tip

Before the round decide:

  • How many mulligans each player gets
  • Whether putts can be replayed
  • If mulligans carry over
  • Whether mulligans can be used after hazards

Criers & Whiners creates nonstop second-guessing and gambling pressure.
