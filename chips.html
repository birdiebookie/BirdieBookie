💵 Chips Game

The ultimate golf side action game. Reward great shots. Punish bad ones. Every hole matters.

How Chips Works

Players earn chips for special accomplishments during the round.

  • Birdies
  • Sandies
  • Greenies
  • Barkies
  • Polies
  • Arnies

Each chip is worth an agreed dollar amount. At the end of the round all chips are totaled and paid out.

Popular Chip Types

Birdie

Score one under par on a hole.

Greenie

Closest to the pin on par 3s and make par or better.

Sandie

Get up and down for par from a bunker.

Barkie

Hit a tree and still make par.

BirdieBookie Tip

Always decide BEFORE the round:

  • Chip values
  • What counts
  • Carryovers
  • Team or individual scoring

Small chip values can still create huge action by the 18th hole.
