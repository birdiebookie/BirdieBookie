Official BirdieBookie Chip Definitions

  • Fairway: 1 on, 3 off = the 3 off owe the fairway player 1 chip each. 3 on, 1 off = the player off owes each fairway player 1 chip. 2 on / 2 off = no blood. 4 on or 4 off = no blood.
  • Greenie: Closest to the pin wins. If nobody hits the green, closest to the pin after the next shot wins.
  • Greenie Tie: If determined to be a true tie, the Greenie pot is split equally.
  • Sandie: Make par or better after hitting from any bunker. Fairway bunkers and greenside bunkers both qualify.
  • Chippie: Hole out from off the green. Fringe counts. Putter may not be used. Does not need to be for par or better.
  • Poley: Make a putt longer than one flagstick length.
  • Double Poley: Make a putt longer than two flagstick lengths.
  • Birdie: Every player who does not make birdie owes 1 chip to each player who does make birdie.
  • Eagle: Worth 5 chips.
  • Barkie: Hit the trunk of a tree and still make par or better. Tree contact from the tee shot does not count.
  • Arnie: Make par or better without ever hitting the fairway.
  • Demon: Activated when 3 players make the exact same score on a hole.
