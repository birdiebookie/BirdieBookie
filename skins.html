🎯 Skins

One hole. One winner. Pure pressure golf gambling where every hole can swing the money.

How Skins Works

Each hole is worth a set dollar amount called a "skin."

To win the skin, a player must win the hole outright.

  • Lowest unique score wins the skin
  • Ties carry the skin over
  • Carryovers can create massive payouts late in the round

Example: If Hole 1 ties, Hole 2 becomes worth 2 skins.

Popular Skins Variations

Carryover Skins

Tied holes roll into the next hole creating larger payouts.

Birdie Skins

Only birdies or better can win a skin.

Team Skins

2 vs 2 format using best ball scoring.

BirdieBookie Tip

Skins gets more intense as carryovers build. A single birdie late in the round can clean out the group.

Before the round decide:

  • Dollar value per skin
  • Whether ties carry over
  • If birdies are required
  • Individual or team format
