🔨 Hammer

Raise the pressure. Raise the stakes. Hammer is one of the most aggressive golf gambling games ever created.

How Hammer Works

Hammer is usually played 2 vs 2.

At any point during the hole, a player can yell:

“HAMMER!”

This doubles the current bet amount instantly.

  • $2 becomes $4
  • $4 becomes $8
  • $8 becomes $16

The opposing team can accept the hammer or sometimes counter-hammer depending on house rules.

Popular Hammer Rules

Air Hammer

Hammer can be called while the ball is still in the air.

Auto Hammer

Closest-to-pin or birdie situations trigger automatic doubles.

Unlimited Hammer

No limit on how many times bets can double during a hole.

BirdieBookie Tip

Hammer escalates FAST. What starts as a small bet can explode by the 18th hole.

Before teeing off decide:

  • Starting bet size
  • Maximum hammer limit
  • Whether air hammers count
  • Whether counter-hammers are allowed
