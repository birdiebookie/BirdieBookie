💰 Nassau

The most classic golf gambling game ever created. Front 9. Back 9. Total score. Simple, dangerous, addictive.

How Nassau Works

A Nassau is traditionally made up of 3 separate bets:

  • Front 9
  • Back 9
  • Total 18

Example: $5 Front $5 Back $5 Total Total exposure = $15.

Players can also "press" during the match to start additional side bets.

Popular Nassau Variations

Automatic Press

Automatically starts a new bet when a team goes 2-down.

2-Man Nassau

Best ball teams compete for front, back and total.

Wolf Nassau

Combine Wolf rotations with Nassau scoring.

BirdieBookie Tip

Nassau games can escalate FAST with presses. Always agree on:

  • Bet size
  • Auto-press rules
  • Carryovers
  • Maximum presses allowed
